3 3 Basic Psychological Needs

Self-Determination Theory identifies three core needs — autonomy, competence, and relatedness — that must be met for motivation to flourish. Understanding these is the foundation for lasting change.

4 Know Your Why

Motivation without meaning fades fast. How to connect your goals to your deeper values so that your "why" carries you through when willpower runs out.

Lasting motivation isn't about trying harder — it's about changing at the right level. Exploring identity, process, and outcome as the three layers that drive sustainable behavior change.
